Welcome to Grandi Woods

Where comfort meets wilderness in the serene village of Vattavada.

  • Thoughtfully designed cottages and a cozy tent stay.
  • Affordable stays with a genuine connection to nature.
  • Misty landscapes, organic local cuisine, and warm hospitality await.

Whether you're planning a quiet escape, a romantic weekend, or a family trip to the hills of Munnar, Grandiwoods offers the perfect setting. We believe in slow travel, sustainable living, and meaningful moments — all set against the breathtaking backdrop of Kerala’s high ranges.
