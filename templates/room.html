About The Cottage

Discover serenity at Grandi Woods, where rustic charm meets modern luxury. Wake to birdsong, sip tea with forest views, and unwind under starry skies.

Nestled in the heart of Vattavada, Grandi Woods Cottage offers a peaceful escape surrounded by rolling hills, misty mornings, and lush vegetable farms. Its wooden interiors, warm ambience, and thoughtfully designed spaces create the perfect blend of tradition and comfort.

Guests can enjoy nature walks, explore nearby trekking trails, or simply relax by the fireplace with a cup of locally brewed tea. Whether you're seeking solitude or a cozy retreat with family and friends, Grandi Woods invites you to experience the untouched beauty of Vattavada in its purest form.

With panoramic views of forests and valleys, every moment at Grandi Woods feels like stepping into a postcard. The cottage is more than just a stay—it's a chance to reconnect with nature, recharge your soul, and take home timeless memories.
